Jeff Ensworth

I am an Environmental Studies undergraduate with an emphasis in Geospatial Analysis pursuing an Environment and Community M.A. at Humboldt State University. My areas of interest include a critical analysis of people's place and relationship with the environment with an emphasis on inclusion and a desire to uphold civil liberties. I am committed to fighting for environmental justice, combating structual inequality and institutional oppression, deconstructing social constructions, and questioning normative claims via a broad historical, political, and cultural lens. Environmental Studies Club Representative.
