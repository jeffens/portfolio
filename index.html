Jeff Ensworth

I am an Environmental Studies undergraduate at Humboldt State University in Arcata, Ca. My areas of interest include a critical analysis of people's place in the environment, with an emphasis on inclusion, and a desire to uphold civil liberties. I am committed to fighting for environmental justice, combating structual inequality and institutional oppression, deconstructing social constructions, questioning normative claims, and challenging the status quo via a broad historical, political, and cultural lens.
